How It Works

Predict. Trade. Win. It's that simple.

Browse Markets

Check out questions about sports, politics, crypto, and more. Each market is a simple YES or NO question about what you think will happen.

Place Your Bet

Think something will happen? Buy YES. Don't think so? Buy NO. The market shows what everyone thinks the chances are.

Example:

• Market at 65% = Most people think YES

• Market at 35% = Most people think NO

Watch Your Money

Track your bets live. If the market moves your way, your potential profit goes up. You can cash out anytime before it ends.

Get Paid

When the event happens, if you picked right, you get paid. Simple as that.

Real Example:

• You buy YES when market is at 65%

• You invest JMD 10,000

• Event happens (YES wins!)

• You get back: JMD 15,385

• Your profit: JMD 5,385 (54% return)

Good to Know

● How Prices Work

Higher percentage = more people think it'll happen. Lower percentage = bigger win if you're right.

● When It Ends

After the event happens, we check the result and pay out the winners. Losers get nothing.

● Your Balance

New users start with JMD 150,000 play money. Use it to learn how everything works.

● Risk vs Reward

Low percentage = risky but big payout. High percentage = safer but smaller payout.
